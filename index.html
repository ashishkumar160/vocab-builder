PREDICTION

Words from the group:

PREDICTION
AUGUR/AUGURY

Example: Conflicts among the various groups do not augur well for the future of the peace talks.

FORECAST

Example: The forecast said that there would be showers in the afternoon.

FORESIGHT/ FORESEE

Example: She had the foresight to prepare herself financially in case of an accident.

FORETELL

Example: The witch foretold that Mary would marry a prince.

PRESAGE

Example: The clouds below the moon presaged rain.

PRESCIENCE

Example: She was prescient - she sold her shares just before the market crashed.

PROGNOSIS

Example: The prognosis is for more people to work part-time in the future.

Notes :

PRE - means before, DICT - means to speak (like DICTATION),
So, PREDICTION means TO SPEAK IN ADVANCE.
